So who am I? Not an easy question to answer. On the one hand, I am a former engineer who left work several years ago to follow God's calling upon my life. But on the other, I have a burden for the Body of Christ, that is, believers in Jesus. But it goes beyond this. I have been led on a path that may seem inexplicable to some, yet curious in certain aspects. Throughout my journey, I have been single-mindedly pursuing truth above all else. I have no interest in pleasing others when it comes to the things of God. Maybe that is why I have been led on a path that has caused me to take stands that would seem controversial: the bankruptcy of modern religion and the grave concern that the Body of Christ is headed for a terrible fate if not suitably warned. I could try to placate others by "toning down" my words. By nature, I am a very diplomatic person. But when it comes to things concerning the Body of Christ, I will speak what needs to be spoken. That is my promise to you.
